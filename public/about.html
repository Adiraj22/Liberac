My dad’s in his 80’s, and he still physically sends money back using cash via an MTO. I want to make that process for him much easier using a digital platform.”

-- Sulu Fiti

“I was born in India, and came to New Zealand as a 9 year old. When my parents needed money to buy a house they had to borrow from family overseas - which cost them a lot in fees. International money transfers provides a tangible way for people to help their family and friends in a time of need. Sending money overseas should be something that’s easy, and cheap to do.”

-- Adiraj Gupta

Wellington, New Zealand
